About Us

Nestler Infra is a premier mining and distribution company with over 15 years of experience in the industry. We specialize in the extraction, processing, and global distribution of high-quality minerals, serving various industries with a commitment to excellence and reliability.

Our operations extend across domestic and international markets, ensuring seamless supply chains and trusted partnerships with leading manufacturers worldwide.

At the core of Nestler Infra is a dedicated team of industry professionals, engineers, and supply chain experts who bring extensive knowledge and expertise to our operations. Our team ensures that every stage of the mining and distribution process meets the highest standards of quality, sustainability, and efficiency.
